Writing a Strong Argumentative Thesis Statement

Interactive Academic Writing Module

Learning Objectives

  • Identify characteristics of a strong argumentative thesis.
  • Differentiate between weak and strong thesis statements.
  • Revise weak thesis statements into arguable claims.
  • Create an original thesis aligned to a prompt.

Estimated Completion Time: 35 minutes

Anatomy of a Strong Thesis Statement

Weak Example: Social media affects teenagers.

Strong Example: Excessive social media use increases teen anxiety and reduces in-person social skills.
